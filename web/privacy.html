Privacy Policy for wordclock.me

Last Updated: January 2026

The Short Version We built this app because we wanted a great simple Clock. We aren't in the business of selling your data. We don't have accounts, we don't store your personal files on our servers, and we don't show ads.

What we collect We use Google Analytics (Firebase) to help us understand how the app is performing. This tells us things like:

  • Which screens are the most popular.
  • What kind of device you’re using (e.g., Android or Windows).
  • If the app crashed and why.

Why we collect it To fix bugs and figure out what features to build next. That’s it.

Third Parties The only third party involved is Google. They process the analytics data for us. You can check out their privacy policy.

Your Control If you change your mind about tracking, you can toggle it off at any time in the Settings menu of the app.

Questions? If you have any questions, feel free to email us at: bramp@wordclock.me
